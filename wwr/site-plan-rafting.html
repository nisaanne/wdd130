Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website will be to broaden our customer reach. With a website, more people will be able to see what our company has to offer, especially people who are new to the white water rafting community. In this way we will be able to grow the company and increase revenue.

Audience

Who are our target customers? Our target customers are men and women between the ages of 18 and 55. What do they want? They want to take a break from everyday life and have a one or two day adventure. What are their needs that aren't being met? They want to be able to quickly see what rafting adventures are available and sign up for a particular trip.
